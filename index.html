About Our Church

We are a community of believers dedicated to following the teachings of Jesus Christ

JESUS, the Only Way Family Church Inc. (JOWFCI) is a Full-Gospel, Non-Profit, Non-Sectarian, JESUS-centered Christian church committed to proclaiming the truth of the Gospel and nurturing a vibrant community of believers. Established and officially registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) in October 2016, JOWFCI stands as a spiritual home for individuals and families seeking to deepen their relationship with Jesus Christ.

Rooted in the foundational belief that “Jesus is the only way to the Father” (John 14:6), the church emphasizes biblical teaching, heartfelt worship, and active discipleship. JOWFCI welcomes people from all walks of life, fostering unity in diversity through faith, fellowship, and service.

As a Full-Gospel ministry, JOWFCI embraces the entirety of the New Testament message—including salvation, healing, deliverance, and the empowering work of the Holy Spirit. Guided by servant leadership and a passion for outreach, the church continues to grow as a beacon of hope and transformation in its community.

Our Beliefs

The foundation of our faith

  • We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • We believe that Jesus Christ is the only Son of God, born of a virgin, who died for our sins, and rose again on the third day.
  • We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and the final authority for faith and life.
  • We believe that salvation is a gift of God's grace received through faith in Jesus Christ alone.
  • We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit, who indwells and empowers believers.
  • We believe in the spiritual unity of all believers in our Lord Jesus Christ.
